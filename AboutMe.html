About Me

I previously went to the University of Kentucky for a Biology degree but quickly realized that field that I wanted to pursue a career in the world of IT. I am an avid gamer that devotes a large majority of my free time outside of studying for certifcations and finishing my degree honing my skills for future tournaments.I play a large genre of games but mostly survival, RPGs, and FPS games as of late but I normally always branch out and go back to old school games. I am just wanting for the next big game to hook me in so I can start dedicating most of my free times towards, but until then I will stick with the classics.

Let us inform our readers so they can get the full experience of each game so they can never miss another legendary game!
